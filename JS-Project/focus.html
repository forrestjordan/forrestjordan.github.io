Focus Areas

Academic

My academic journey has been driven by a passion for integrating creativity and technology to tackle complex challenges and craft meaningful user experiences. At the ATLAS Institute, I’ve concentrated on product design, computer vision, and UX/UI, participating in a wide array of academic projects that encourage creative problem-solving and technical approaches.

In developing software and interactive systems, I primarily use Python and Arduino C++, while Adobe Creative Cloud and Ableton 12 are my go-to tools for multimedia work. To bring concepts to life and design engaging user experiences, I rely on SketchUp, Rhino, and Figma for 3D modeling and UX/UI design.

I am particularly interested in exploring opportunities within software, consumer electronics, typography, and footwear technology.

Life

Outside of the classroom, I am committed to community service and volunteering. I have launched several projects in Japan focused on fostering intercultural understanding among youth, and I’ve participated in international volunteer initiatives aimed at promoting cultural exchange, most recently in Qingdao, China.

In addition to my volunteer work, I co-founded JAM Data, a startup that provides a comprehensive data management solution for the skydiving industry. Our program streamlines everything from the waiver process to checkout, manifesting, video handling, and finances.

Creatively, I enjoy exploring black-and-white photography, videography, and digital art, each offering a unique way to experiment with visual storytelling.

Physical activity also plays a significant role in my life. I’m an avid cyclist, skier, climber, and basketball player, and I regularly train in muay thai.
